## Introduction

Baobab powder, with its slightly tangy and fruity taste, easily integrates into many recipes. Rich in vitamin C, fiber and antioxidants, it transforms your ordinary dishes into nutritious superfoods. At Zoahary, our 100% pure and organic baobab powder retains all its natural benefits. Discover 5 simple recipes to enjoy this Malagasy treasure daily.

## 1. Energizing Baobab Smoothie

**Ingredients**:
- 1 banana
- 1 cup plant-based milk (almond, coconut or oat)
- 2 tablespoons baobab powder
- 1 tablespoon almond butter
- 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
- A few ice cubes

**Preparation**: Blend all ingredients until smooth and creamy. This drink provides energy and vitality to start your day right.

## 2. Super Vitamin Breakfast Bowl

**Ingredients**:
- 1 plain or plant-based yogurt
- 2 tablespoons baobab powder
- 1 tablespoon granola
- Fresh seasonal fruits (mango, kiwi, berries)
- 1 teaspoon chia seeds
- A drizzle of honey

**Preparation**: Mix yogurt with baobab powder. Arrange in a bowl and garnish with fresh fruits, granola and chia seeds. A complete and delicious breakfast!

## 3. Homemade Baobab Lemonade

**Ingredients** (for 1 liter):
- 3 tablespoons baobab powder
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 2-3 tablespoons honey or agave syrup
- 1 liter fresh water
- Fresh mint leaves

**Preparation**: Mix baobab powder with lemon juice and honey in a pitcher. Add cold water and stir well. Add mint and some ice cubes. A refreshing and vitamin-rich drink perfect for summer!

## 4. Baobab Energy Balls

**Ingredients** (for 12 balls):
- 1 cup pitted dates
- 1/2 cup almonds or cashews
- 3 tablespoons baobab powder
- 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
- 1 pinch of salt
- Shredded coconut for coating

**Preparation**: Blend dates, nuts, baobab powder, cocoa and salt until you get a sticky paste. Form balls and roll them in coconut. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. Healthy and energetic snacks!

## 5. Baobab Vinaigrette Dressing

**Ingredients**:
- 1 tablespoon baobab powder
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon mustard
- 1 teaspoon honey
- Salt and pepper

**Preparation**: Mix all ingredients in a closed jar and shake vigorously. This tangy and nutritious dressing enhances your salads!

## Usage Tips

- Start with 1 teaspoon of baobab powder and gradually increase to 2-3 tablespoons per day.
- Baobab powder mixes better in slightly acidic liquids (fruit juices, yogurt).
- Store your powder in a dry place away from light.
- Do not heat baobab powder to preserve vitamin C.
