## Introduction

The baobab is more than a majestic tree — it's a nutritional powerhouse. At Zoahary we transform baobab pulp and oil into organic products that respect local know-how. In this article, discover 10 scientifically supported benefits and practical tips to add baobab to your routine.

## 1. Rich in Vitamin C

Baobab pulp contains up to 6 times more vitamin C than an orange. This vitamin helps strengthen the immune system and improves iron absorption.

## 2. High in Antioxidants

The antioxidants found in baobab protect cells from oxidative stress and premature aging.

## 3. Promotes Digestion

Rich in soluble fiber, baobab powder supports healthy digestion and a balanced gut microbiome.

## 4. Hydration and Skin Care

Baobab oil is rich in essential fatty acids and vitamins, excellent for hydrating and repairing dry skin.

## 5. Sustainable Energy Source

Natural carbohydrates and micronutrients in baobab provide steady energy — ideal for active people.

## 6. Supporting Local Communities

By choosing Zoahary you support a local, sustainable value chain in Madagascar that creates jobs and protects the environment.

## 7. Easy to Use

Add a spoonful of powder to your smoothie, or use baobab oil as a nourishing hair and body treatment.
